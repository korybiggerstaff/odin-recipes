Indian Chicken Curry

Description

With this simple recipe, you can enjoy flavorful, aromatic chicken curry in under an hour!

No need to leave the country to taste it--or even your house, for that matter!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saut√© onion until lightly browned.
  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt. Continue stirring for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
